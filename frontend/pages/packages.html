Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely! All our packages are flexible. We also offer fully custom packages to match your specific needs and budget.

What if I need services beyond the included hours?

We offer additional service hours at hourly rates. Discuss your needs with us, and we'll find the perfect solution.

Do you include decor materials in the package?

Materials are typically quoted separately based on your design and preferences. We'll provide detailed pricing during consultation.

What's your booking process?

Submit an inquiry, schedule a consultation, review your custom proposal, and sign our agreement to secure your date.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 8-12 weeks in advance for weddings and 4-6 weeks for other events to ensure your preferred dates.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans. Discuss options during your consultation.
